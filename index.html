FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with performance marketing.

We specialize in e-commerce brands, lead generation companies, and DTC businesses doing $500K to $20M in annual revenue. Our sweet spot is companies that have proven product-market fit and are ready to scale profitably through paid channels.
For our Starter plan, we recommend a minimum of $3,000/month in ad spend to generate meaningful data and results. For Performance and Enterprise plans, we typically work with budgets of $10K-$100K+ per month across platforms.
Most clients see measurable improvements within the first 30 days as we implement quick wins from the audit. Significant performance gains typically come in months 2-3 as our optimization cycles compound. We set clear 30/60/90-day benchmarks so you always know where you stand.
We recommend a 3-month initial commitment to allow enough time for proper setup, testing, and optimization. After that, all engagements are month-to-month. We believe our results should earn your continued partnership, not a contract.
We manage Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, Performance Max), Microsoft/Bing Ads, programmatic platforms (DV360, The Trade Desk), Meta Ads, and LinkedIn Ads. Our core expertise is in search and programmatic, where data-driven optimization has the biggest impact.
Our audit covers account structure review, wasted spend analysis, keyword and audience assessment, tracking/attribution gap identification, competitor benchmarking, and a prioritized action plan with projected ROI. It is a comprehensive document you can use whether or not you work with us.
Every client gets a real-time dashboard (Looker Studio or custom) with the metrics that matter to their business. We provide weekly optimization summaries, monthly deep-dive reports, and quarterly strategic reviews. Full transparency on spend, performance, and the actions we are taking.
